# Code of Conduct & Acceptable Use Policy

## OmniFlow is for Personal/Educational Use Only

### ✅ Acceptable Uses

- Personal, non-commercial download of content you own or have permission for
- Educational research and analysis
- Archive/backup of your own content

### ❌ Prohibited Uses

- **Commercial use** of any downloaded content without copyright holder permission
- **Reselling** or republishing downloaded media
- **Automated harvesting** at scale without platform permission
- **Circumventing authentication** or platform restrictions
- **Violating platform ToS**

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Your content/account being banned from target platforms
- Legal action from copyright holders
- Civil/criminal penalties depending on jurisdiction

**We will not intervene. You are on your own.**

## Platform-Specific Notes

Each platform has specific ToS restrictions:

- **YouTube**: Prohibits downloading without permission (except via official tools)
- **TikTok**: Prohibits scraping/downloading
- **Instagram**: Prohibits automated access
- **LinkedIn**: Prohibits content harvesting
- **Twitter/X**: Prohibits bulk content download
- **Facebook**: Prohibits scraping

**Check ToS of each platform before use.**

---

If you disagree with these terms, don't use OmniFlow.
